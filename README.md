# IBM-Cognos-Analytics-Dashboard-Project
Built an interactive IBM Cognos Analytics dashboard featuring sales and profit analysis, dealer performance tracking, customer sentiment insights, and recall investigations, demonstrating business intelligence, data visualization, and reporting skills.

Overview-

This project was developed as part of my learning journey in IBM Cognos Analytics. Through this hands-on project, I explored how to transform raw business data into meaningful visualizations and interactive dashboards that support data-driven decision-making.

The project helped me strengthen my understanding of data visualization, dashboard design, reporting, and business analytics while gaining practical experience with IBM Cognos Analytics.


Dashboard Highlights-
Profit & Sales Performance
Analyzed overall business performance using key metrics such as profit and units sold.
Dashboard reports a total profit of $78.4M with more than 58K units sold.
Provides a quick overview of business growth and revenue contribution.
Dealer Performance Analysis
Visualized profit distribution across different dealer IDs.
Identified top-performing dealers and potential areas for performance improvement.
Enabled comparison of dealer profitability through interactive charts.
Vehicle Recall Analysis
Examined recall counts across vehicle models.
Models such as Beaufort and Labrador displayed higher recall frequencies.
Helped uncover potential quality and manufacturing concerns.
Monthly Sales & Profit Trends
Combined profit and quantity sold metrics to analyze monthly performance.
Revealed seasonal patterns and fluctuations in demand.
Supports forecasting and strategic business planning.
Customer Sentiment Analysis
Used a Tree Map visualization to analyze customer feedback.
Positive sentiment represented the majority of responses.
Highlighted areas where customer experience improvements could reduce neutral and negative feedback.
System-Level Recall Investigation
Created a heatmap to identify frequently affected vehicle systems.
Compared recall patterns across models and components such as engines, brakes, and other systems.
Supports targeted maintenance and quality improvement initiatives.
